Competitive Video Gaming and eSports Tournaments by CMG

Check Mate Gaming (CMG) is a network where you can use your video game skills to compete online in tournaments for money. This competitive video gaming hub is a must-visit for any players who have a flair for decimating their opponents in first-person shooters (FPS).

Tournaments, Wagers and Ladders

CMG will offer league and leaderboard challenges across a selection of the fiercest eSports titles on the face of the planet and beyond. The Call of Duty: Modern Warfare series has remained the pinnacle among FPS players for years, but now there is a new series waiting to be conquered.

Here at CMG, you can also play games for money while blasting your way through competitive gaming tournaments in Call of Duty: Infinite Warfare. This new branch of Call of Duty takes the tremendously precise FPS gameplay that players love and blasts out of Earth's atmosphere to take the fight into the universe.

Compete for Cash

CMG has streamlined the process of eSports by engineering a platform that can seamlessly connect you with ladder competitions and wager matches from people all over the world. After creating a CMG account, you can use the network to start playing for cash prizes.

Individual tournaments are simple for finding opportunities to seek out earnings with your FPS skillset. What's also impressive is that you can use your account to find a team of like-minded soldiers of fortune to go out and earn a prize. There are so many daily winning chances to get out there and play for cash, so don't wait around.

CMG is also designed to help you find tournaments for your preferred gaming platform. This network can deliver whether you want PS4, Xbox One and PC options. CMG is all about making it easier for you to parlay your FPS abilities into money-making opportunities.
